Gray Ave

This complex comprises 32 Studios, within four seperate titles consisting of 8-bedroom dwellings. The complex is located on the main road adjacent to Middlemore Hospital. This complex was built to service the accommodation needs of the many hospital workers in the area. The complex has run at 95% occupancy since opening in 2012.

Rosella Road

This complex comprises 46 Studios consisting of six titles of 7-8 dwellings. The complex borders the Middlemore Hospital grounds. Completed in 2015, it serves the accommodation needs of Middlemore Hospital staff, who can access the site through a secure gate connecting the two properties.

Carruth Road

This site contains 27 Studios within three seperate titles of 9-bedroom dwellings. The complex is being built to service the accommodation needs of those at the nearby Manukau town centre, or studying at the two tertiary institutions in close proximity.

James Road

This site contains 27 Studios within three separate titles of 9-bedroom dwellings. The complex is located very close to the Manurewa town centre and train station. It is being built to service the accommodation needs of the many workers in the area, particularly in Manurewa township. Located nearby are many shops, restaurants and the Manurewa train station, which are all within walking distance.

Plunket Avenue

This site contains 36 Studios within four separate titles of 9-bedroom dwellings. Completed in 2015, it was built to attract tenants working at the nearby Manukau town centre, or studying at one of the several tertiary institutions in the area. Located nearby are shops, restaurants and bus stops as well as the Puhinui train station, all within walking distance.

Puhinui Road

Completed in 2015, the complex contains 16 Studios within two separate titles of 8-bedroom dwellings. As it lies between the Manukau town centre and Auckland International Airport, it was built to cater specifically to airport staff. Located nearby are shops, restaurants and bus stops as well as the Puhinui train station, all within walking distance.
