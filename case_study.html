Projects

Chapel studios

Great location, great returns

15 units / 75 studios

The site has been strategically selected to serve the booming East Auckland rental market, and is located within close distance of the gorgeous Fo Guang Shan Buddhist Temple, Botany Junction and the Botany Big Box Shopping Centre, the influx of assets to the area is assured.

Eastern promises

It began when we found the right investors to get the ball rolling. Clarke Group had long wanted to develop something in the Eastern Suburbs. We chose land strategically based on proximity to infrastructure.

Previous returns garnered by Clarke Group brought one investor back to work with us, while the results we had achieved from previous projects brought in a second, new investor. They saw Clarke Group’s vision, passion, and determination and knew they were onto a winner.



Following our development dream

Getting through resource consent was a long process. The Chapel Studios design was the first of its nature for us, meaning the project had to prove itself to town planners. We invested in a legal expert well versed in resource management law who was as passionate as we were about getting the project approved.

The challenge came in the decision to use horizontal titling, with one title stacked on top of each other, on top of another. As one of our first three storey developments, we’re able to offer 50 percent more accommodation than our previous two storey developments – and generate the benefits that come from that.

Persistency and determination were what it took.
We knew we had a winning product and we fought to get our vision on the market.
Finally, approval was granted to obtain our trophy –
and we look forward to letting you hold it with us.
