Meet the team

Passionate about property, passionate about people

With a proven track record, and several more projects on the go, Clarke Group’s success comes down to one thing – our people. We’re proud to have a team of smart, experienced people who truly care about working together to get the best results for investors and tenants alike.

Austen Clarke

Director

You could say that property development is in Austen’s blood – and with his introduction to it coming from his mum in his late teens, it is perhaps unsurprising that Clarke Group is doing what it’s doing today.

Starting his property development career in Hamilton, New Zealand, in 2001, Austen has years of property development experience – with many successes and the odd failure along the way providing him with countless lessons for what works and what doesn’t.

He took these lessons and wrote the thesis for his MBA (Distinction) on the feasibility of launching this model in Auckland before founding the Clarke Group in 2013.

A strong believer in surrounding yourself with smart people, he soon bought on broad skilled and trusted team members to work alongside him and challenge him to always do even better.

Chris Sutherland General Manager

Part of Clarke Group since virtually its inception, Chris has previously managed complex, highly technical refits of super yachts to exacting standards in the shipyards of France and Italy. He brings that project management experience to Clarke Group, where he oversees project developments and property management.

Sam Martin Accountant

A seasoned accountant, Sam provides all internal accounting functions for the Clarke Group. With a degree in accounting and finance, and Xero certification, Sam has huge experience in preparing and analysing financial information across various industry sectors.

Josh Stevenson Project Manager

Josh has over 20 years in the construction, development and planning industry, from starting his work life on the tools right through to management roles across government, commercial, residential, and private sectors.

Natalia Rozova Project Coordinator

With a strong background in marketing and product development, and a Masters in marketing, Natalia advises on all marketing initiatives and oversees software development of a Clarke Group start-up.

Chris Scott Business Development Manager

After eight years in managerial roles within the property investment and development advisory sector in Sydney, Chris brings a depth of knowledge and experience to his role dealing with site acquisition and expanding new and existing investor relations.

Baptist Lobo Assistant Account

A Charted Accountant and CPA (Australia), Baptist has more than 20 years of finance and accounting experience, including in multinational companies across the world. Baptist strengthens our accounting team, providing reporting guidance and strong data analysis.

Anna Batuita PA to the Director

Anna Batiuta joined Clarke Group in 2017 to support management in organising and coordinating their daily meetings and office. Anna comes from Ukraine and has a management and personal assistant background as well as a specialist degree in Linguistics and a bachelor degree in Law.

Jerry Faifua Property Manager

With a strong background in customer service and a passion for exceeding in life, Jerry recently joined the property management arm of Clarke Group to ensure properties are well-maintained, tenants are looked after, and everyone is kept happy.

Zhongdao Hu (Hugh) International Marketing Specialist

Originally from China, Hugh joined the Clarke Group in our early stages of expansion and has been an instrumental part of internationalising Clarke Group and opening up the business to foreign equity investment, particularly from the Chinese market.

Sammy Carleton Property Manager

The first point of contact for all new rental enquiries, Sammy has over 10 years’ retail experience, and the customer service skills to show for it. She now assists our growing property management division that manages nearly 200 studios across Auckland.
